How do you set up a pretty picnic?

Before:

  • Have a group of friends
  • Plan what food each one will bring
  • Choose a good place
  • Choose a comfortable outfit
  • Apply sunscreen and repellent
  • Put the food in a container

Set up:

  • Locate in the previously chosen place
  • Meet with friends
  • Fix the tablecloth
  • Set up the pinic baskets
  • Accommodate food and refreshments
  • Prepare for the banquet

During

  • Take some photos
  • Enjoy the atmosphere
  • Eat!
  • have a nice chat
  • Have fun
  • Don't forget to clean
